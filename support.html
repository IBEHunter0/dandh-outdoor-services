How We Work

We keep things simple. You tell us what you need done, we ask a few questions if necessary, and we schedule a time that works for you. Prices depend on yard size and how much work there is. If a job ends up being more work than expected, we’ll let you know before continuing.

What We Offer

D&H Services focuses on practical, straightforward yard work and cleanups. This includes:

  • Lawn mowing and raking
  • Garage or shed cleanup
  • Trimming bushes and bagging leaves
  • Watering and weed pulling
  • Backyard furniture cleanup

We don’t do large landscaping projects, stonework, or construction. If that’s what you’re looking for, a bigger company may be better suited.
